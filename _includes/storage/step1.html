Get a backup. Make sure your storage system is backed up (reword). This could range from an external hard drive to a storage system managed by your institution's IT department.

Get things off heterogeneous media and onto a storage system. ‘Heterogeneous media’ might be floppy discs, CD-Rs, DVD-Rs, internal or external hard drives, and thumb or flash drives. These aren’t appropriate storage spaces for digital preservation. Read and follow OCLC’s You’ve Got to Walk Before You Can Run: First Steps for Managing Born-Digital Content Received on Physical Media.
